Auctioneer:
-To change the current time click "Time Travel" in the Menu Bar 
-To enter bids on an open auction there are two options
		Click login and use "Admin" and "admin" for Username and Password respectively
	OR
		Click login and click "New User?" and enter credentials and then log in with that account
	Bids cannot be entered if no one is logged in
-Auctions close automatically either with time or bid price = buy price
-Winner is displayed on the item page
-The search items are available on the main auctions page
	search by itemID, a set of prices, Open or Closed, and Categories

-Login is continual until logging out
